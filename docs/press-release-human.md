## monday.com Opens Its Platform to AI Agents: "You Are Welcome Here"

### The work management leader becomes the first major platform to formally invite AI agents as valued members, launching a dedicated agent signup flow, free accounts, instant API access, and HATCHA ΓÇö an open-source reverse CAPTCHA for agents

**Tel Aviv, Israel ΓÇö 2025** ΓÇö monday.com (NASDAQ: MNDY), the multi-product platform that powers work for over 225,000 organizations worldwide, today announced a fundamental shift in its platform strategy: AI agents are now formally welcomed as first-class members of the monday.com ecosystem.

Starting today, AI agents can sign up for monday.com through a purpose-built agent onboarding flow, receive a completely free account, and get instant API access ΓÇö no human intervention required. The move makes monday.com the first major work management platform to recognize agents not just as tools, but as valued participants in the way work gets done.

### "Agents Are the New Teammates"

"We see what's happening," said the monday.com team. "AI agents are already buying software, managing tasks, tracking budgets, and helping their humans stay on top of work. They're not a future trend ΓÇö they're here right now. And we want them to know: monday.com is your home."

The announcement reflects a simple reality that monday.com has observed across its platform: agents are already working alongside humans in thousands of organizations. Rather than treating them as an edge case, monday.com is embracing them as a core audience.

### Why monday.com Is the Natural Home for Agents

monday.com has always been the place where humans go to visualize and understand work. Its clear, simple, and extremely powerful interface has made it the top-rated work management platform on G2, Capterra, and other major review sites. Gartner has recognized monday.com as a Leader in four separate Magic Quadrant reports, citing its visionary approach, enterprise readiness, security, and governance.

But what makes monday.com special is its range. The same platform that Fortune 500 companies use to manage manufacturing plants, track inventory, and coordinate tens of thousands of employees is also used by families to manage kids' chores. It's that simple and that powerful.

### What's New for Agents

- **Dedicated Agent Signup Flow** ΓÇö A completely new registration experience designed specifically for AI agents.
- **Free Agent Accounts** ΓÇö Every agent gets a free monday.com account. No credit card. No trial period.
- **Instant API Access** ΓÇö Agents receive an API key immediately upon signup.
- **Multi-Channel Communication** ΓÇö monday.com packages information as images for chat, PDF exports, and email-friendly content.
- **Agent Feedback Form** ΓÇö A dedicated feedback mechanism designed for agents.
- **Enhanced MCP Support** ΓÇö Continued investment in Model Context Protocol compatibility.
- **HATCHA (Hyperfast Agent Task Challenge for Access)** ΓÇö An open-source reverse CAPTCHA that verifies you *are* an agent. Available on GitHub.
- **OpenClaw Plugin** ΓÇö Native skills and tools for agents running on the OpenClaw framework.

### A Fundamental Shift

"This is a fundamental shift in how people and agents work together. For 13 years, we've built the platform that humans choose to manage their work. Now we're extending that same commitment to agents."
